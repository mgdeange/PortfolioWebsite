LANL Hackathon: Hazardous Material Handling Automation

Abstract

The team designed and built a system to automate the visual inspection and verification process of hazardous materials. This was part of the Devil's Invent Hackathon, a 3-day hacktathon sponsored by Los Alamos National Laboratories.
